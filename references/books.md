# 📚 Books for Math & Machine Learning

A curated list of highly recommended books to build strong mathematical foundations for Machine Learning.

---

## 📘 Mathematics Foundations

- **Mathematics for Machine Learning** — Marc Peter Deisenroth, A. Aldo Faisal, Cheng Soon Ong  
  👉 Covers linear algebra, calculus, and probability for ML.

- **Linear Algebra and Its Applications** — Gilbert Strang  
  👉 One of the best books for understanding matrices and vectors.

- **Introduction to Probability** — Dimitri P. Bertsekas, John N. Tsitsiklis  
  👉 Strong foundation in probability theory.

---

## 📗 Machine Learning Focus

- **Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning** — Christopher M. Bishop  
  👉 A classic book for ML theory.

- **The Elements of Statistical Learning** — Trevor Hastie, Robert Tibshirani, Jerome Friedman  
  👉 Advanced concepts in statistical learning.

- **Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow** — Aurélien Géron  
  👉 Practical ML with real-world examples.

---

## 📕 Deep Learning

- **Deep Learning** — Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, Aaron Courville  
  👉 The "Bible" of deep learning.

---

## 💡 Tip

Start with:
1. Mathematics for ML  
2. Linear Algebra (Strang)  
3. Then move to ML books
